About us

We are students in the department of Computer Science at the University of Victoria. As students in CSC 578A: Topics in Software Applications: "Computer-Supported Collaborative Work", we are required to evaluate a software solution as part of this course. So, we choose to develop this project to test the applicability of the MoCA framework.
